# Data

## `early-sessions.json`

Hardcoded conference session records for 1830–1847.

The LDS Church did not hold regular semiannual conferences in this period. Sessions were called
as needed, sometimes quarterly, sometimes more frequently. The exact dates for some early
sessions are uncertain — they represent the best available information from:

- Joseph Smith Papers Calendar of Documents
- Minute Book 1 and 2 (JSP)
- History of the Church

**Known omissions:**
- Some 1832–1835 conferences that appear in the historical record only as brief minutes
  without full dates have been approximated

**1846:** No conference was held. The Saints began their westward exodus in February 1846.

Sessions from 1848 onwards are generated programmatically by the seed script, with the
following special cases:
- **October 1957**: Cancelled due to the Asian flu epidemic.
- **April 1880**: Included in the `1850-1879` era as the "Jubilee Conference."
